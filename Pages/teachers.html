About us

Business Advisory is an English Institute that started its services in 2020 with the only purpose of satisfying the English learning needs of individuals, local and international companies by providing them with the necessary support to face the challenges and concerns of everyday life and the emerging global market. Professionalism, flexibility and creativity are our uttermost values and aims.
