Courses

ESP

Specifically designed to meet the needs and goals that each work area demands.

For kids

The use of realia (toys, pictures and drawings) and fun become the teacher's main teaching aid and ally at this early stage of the learning process.

For teenagers

Though the use of extracts from company brochures, manuals, diagrams, drawings and oral presentations students become the main and only protagonist of their own learning process.

For adults

These courses area designed and carefully paced to help adults meet their learning needs. Their likes, dislikes, difficulties and challenges are the core of each program.

Conversation

These courses are designed to encourage students to speak and build up vocabulary in order to be able to actively participate in discussions, negotiations and conversations which, directly or indirectly, involve the use of the English language.

Job interview preparations

It consist of short mini-sessions to enhance the strengths and weaknesses of each individual when facing a job interview.
